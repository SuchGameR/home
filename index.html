An aside to the creation of this site


Hi, I'm SGR.
Have you ever written commands or source code?
I am familiar with code since I was a child.
I built this website and published it, and most of you would be "wow"

But I myself used to, of course, not be able to source code or even write commands.
But I go to foreign websites and foreign YouTube. So, what I realized is, "If you do the same thing every day, you will get used to it.

What about?" Isn't it obvious?" but it is very important.
It is the same for studying, and when you practice or learn something, you learn it by repeating it.
The same is true for programming languages.
It may sound difficult, but there are quite a few people who think programming languages are difficult.
It is not that programs are difficult, but that it is difficult to "think about the mechanism of processing" of a program.

Most of the processing of a program is "processing", "conditional branching", and "repetition"

Processing allows you to give commands to the computer
Conditional branching allows the computer to determine if the conditions match.
Repetition allows the computer to determine how many times to repeat the process

Some programs are difficult in their syntax, but I don't want you to mistake them for something that can never be done!
In this day and age, IT is advancing rapidly. Although some convenient things are coming out, I think it is also important to think creatively so that you can "create your own" inconvenient things.

What I am trying to say here is, "Never give up" and "How can I change things?" I wrote this in my own way here.
I hope that my thoughts will reach you even through the Internet.
